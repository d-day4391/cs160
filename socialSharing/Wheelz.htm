Description

This is a great Northern California car. It has a convenient hatchback, the seats fold down easily for storing extra gear and it has a gear / bike rack on the roof. The stereo has iPod an connection and USB. It has seen its share of great adventures and is ready for more. I really love this car, so please take good care of it.

Special Instructions

    Stereo:
    - iPod connection is installed through the glove compartment (it's unfortunately incompatible with iPhones)

    Other:
